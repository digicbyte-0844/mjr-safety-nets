How do I book an installation service?

Booking is easy! Simply call us at 9553331979 or WhatsApp us. Our team will schedule a free site visit, provide a quote, and complete the installation at your convenience. We serve all areas across Hyderabad.

What types of safety nets do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range including: Pigeon Safety Nets, Anti-Bird Nets, Balcony Safety Nets, Children Safety Nets, Construction Safety Nets, Cricket Practice Nets, Industrial Safety Nets, Building Safety Nets, and many more specialized solutions.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations (balconies, windows) take 2-4 hours depending on the area size. Commercial and construction site installations may take longer. We ensure quick, efficient installation with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

What is the lifespan of your safety nets?

Our UV-resistant HDPE safety nets have a lifespan of 5-7 years with proper maintenance. The nets are weather-resistant and designed to withstand harsh conditions including rain, sun, and wind.

Are the nets visible from outside?

Our safety nets are designed to be nearly invisible. We use transparent and color-matched nets that blend seamlessly with your building's aesthetic while providing effective protection.

Do you provide warranty on installation?

Yes! We provide a 2-year warranty on professional installation and workmanship. Our high-quality materials come with a 5-7 year durability guarantee. We stand behind our work with 100% satisfaction assurance.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden costs. Prices vary based on net type, area size, and height. Contact us for a free quote and site visit. We guarantee the best quality at the most affordable rates in Hyderabad.

Can nets be installed on all types of buildings?

Absolutely! We install safety nets on all types of residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Whether it's a high-rise apartment, independent house, commercial complex, or construction site, we have customized solutions for every property type.

Do pigeon nets harm birds?

No, our pigeon nets are completely humane. They simply create a physical barrier that prevents birds from entering your space without causing any harm. It's a safe, ethical solution for bird control that protects both your property and the birds.
